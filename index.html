The rockers


Sound of Silence


The Rockers are a music band that plays rock and roll with a twist of funk and soul. They have been performing since 2020 and have released three albums so far: Rock On, Funky Soul, and Rock the World. The band consists of four members: Jack on vocals and guitar, Lily on bass and backup vocals, Tom on drums, and Anna on keyboard and saxophone. They are known for their energetic live shows, catchy songs, and diverse influences. The Rockers have won several awards, including the Best New Artist at the Music Awards 2021 and the Best Rock Band at the Music Festival 2022. They are currently working on their fourth album, which is expected to be released in 2023.The Rockers are one of the most popular and innovative music bands in the industry today.
